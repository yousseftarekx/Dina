MUSIC GOES IN THIS FOLDER
=========================

The player is already built and wired up. It just needs the actual song
files. I can't include them because they're copyrighted, so grab your own
copies (you probably already have them) and drop them in here with these
EXACT names:

    khalik-maaya.mp3       ->  Khalik Maaya  (Amr Diab)
    yama-sawa.mp3          ->  Yama Sawa      (Cairokee)
    msh-kol-marra.mp3      ->  Msh Kol Marra  (Wegz & Nasser)

That's it. Refresh the page and the player plays them, in order, with
working play / pause / next / previous / volume / seek / song picker,
and it keeps playing while she moves between pages.

Want different songs, more songs, or different names?
Open  js/data.js  and edit the SONGS list near the top. The player builds
itself from whatever you put there.

Note: .mp3 is the safest format for browsers. .m4a and .ogg usually work
too. Avoid weird formats.
